Characteristics of a Tech Hub

  • 🏢 Co-working Space: Open workspaces designed for startups, developers, and entrepreneurs.
  • 💡 Innovation & Creativity: New ideas are supported and turned into practical, scalable projects.
  • 🤝 Networking Opportunities: Meetups, workshops, and tech conferences where professionals connect and collaborate.
  • 💰 Investment Opportunities: Venture capitalists and investors are present, looking to fund promising ideas.
  • 👨‍💻 Tech Training & Events: Activities like coding bootcamps, AI training, and entrepreneurship programs are common.
